Let's celebrate Software Freedom Day

Software Freedom Day (SFD) is a worldwide celebration of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS). Our goal in this celebration which remembers the benefits of free and open software and promotes its use. Every third Saturday of September, this date is celebrated by thousands of people all around the planet, who reminds us the importance of sharing knowledge in computing field. SFD is a public education effort with the aim of increasing awareness of Free Software and its virtues, and encouraging its use.

Software Freedom Day (SFD) is all about.

Free to Use

The Freedom to run the program as you wish for any purpose.

Free to Study

The Freedom to study how the program works, and modify it.

Free to Distribute

The Freedom to redistribute copies so you can help your neighbour.

Free to Modify

The Freedom to distribute copies of your modified versions of others

How Can We Help

Taking FOSS to the People? Yes, The primary step in that would be the Institutions. FSFTN conducts College Workshops, One Day Sessions, Guest Lectures, etc to reach the College Students and invite them to work on Projects, Community Initiatives, etc.

GNU/Linux Users Group (GLUG) are the essential place for Knowledge sharing activities for students from Colleges and Regions co-ordinated by Cuddalore GLUG.

Community Projects are the best way to directly address social issues, needs, etc through FOSS Tools and Technologies. FSFTN also guides College students in their Final Year projects towards Community Requirements and Contributions.

Little bit about ourself

Cuddalore GLUG is a not-for-profit organization and a social movement which aims at enlightening the masses in Free Software, Openness, Free Culture and Knowledge Freedom.

We believe in a world where every child can read. Our mission is to invest in early childhood education in order to empower the next generation. We do that, by creating educational programs and providing necessary resources in underprivileged areas. We believe in smart fund allocation, and therefore we employ minimal staff.
